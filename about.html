History

The history of the Olympics includes ancient games in Greece, a period of decline, and the revival of the modern Games in 1896.

Olympics

Baron Pierre de Coubertin of France founded the International Olympic Committee (IOC) in 1894. The Olympic symbol, the Olympic rings, were designed by Pierre de Coubertin in 1913.

Vision

The Olympics have been impacted by the Cold War, with political boycotts in 1956, 1980, and 1984.
